What Is MUN?

MUN (Model United Nations) is a conference-like competition where one plays the role of a diplomat. You could be nation's delegate, an important historical/goverment figure, or even representatives of foreign bodies;NGOs, press, etc. to discuss regarding vatious topics and problems with othe participants and create a draft resolution
